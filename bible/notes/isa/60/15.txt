===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **with no one passing through you**  - AT: "with everyone ignoring your land" or "with all the foreigners ignoring your land" (See: [[:kn:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **You will also drink the milk of the nations, and will nurse at the breast of kings**  - This refers to the wealth that will be drained from foreign nations. (See: [[:kn:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **Redeemer**  - See how you translated this in [[:kn:bible:notes:isa:41:14|41:14]].
  * **the Mighty One of Jacob**  - See how you translated this in [[:kn:bible:notes:isa:49:26|49:26]].
