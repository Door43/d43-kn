===== Translation Notes: =====

The people of Israel continue speaking.

  * **As the cattle that go down into the valley**  - This may refer to cattle going into a valley where there is green grass and water. This emphasizes that God led the people of Israel and took care of them. (See: [[:kn:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **to make yourself a name of praise**  - Here "name of praise" refers to honor and one's reputation. AT: "so you would have an honored name" (See: [[:kn:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
