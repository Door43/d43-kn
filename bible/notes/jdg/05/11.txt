===== Translation Notes: =====

The song of Deborah and Barak continues.

  * **Hear the voices of those who divide up the sheep between the watering places** - This describes singers who would tell stories through song.  They stood between the herds of sheep drinking water at different pools and sang for the people who gathered there.
