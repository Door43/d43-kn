===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **send rain on the land** - AT: "cause rain to fall on the land" (See: [[:kn:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **now the famine was severe** - The word "now" is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here the writer tells background information about how the famine had affected Samaria. (See: [[kn:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
