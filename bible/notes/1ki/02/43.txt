===== Translation Notes: =====

King Solomon pronounces judgment upon Shimei for leaving Jerusalem.

  * **on your own head**  - AT: "on you"; Here "head" refers to the whole person. (See: [[:kn:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
