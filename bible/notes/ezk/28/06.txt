===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the ruler of Tyre.
  * **you have made your heart like the heart of a god** - See how you translated "you make your heart like the heart of a god" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:28:01|28:2]].
  * **against you** - "who will attack you"
  * **terrifying men from other nations** - "men who make the nations very afraid"
  * **will bring their swords** - "will draw their swords"
  * **the beauty of your wisdom** - AT: "the beautiful city that you have built with you wisdom." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **wisdom** - In this passage, "wisdom" is "intelligence" or "the ability to achieve something through careful planning."
  * **will profane** - Yahweh uses the word "profane" because the prince of Tyre thinks that he is holy like a god.
