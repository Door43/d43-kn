===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:kn:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:kn:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David** - This can also be written as a sentence: “This is a psalm of David“ or “This is a psalm that David wrote.”
  * **whom should I fear?** - This question emphasizes that there is no one that David should fear. AT: "There is no one that I should be afraid of." (See: [[:kn:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **whom should I dread?** - This question emphasizes that there is no one that David should be afraid of". AT: "There is no one that I should dread" (See: [[:kn:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
