===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **SHIN:**  - This is the twenty-first letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 161-168 begins with this letter.
  * **my heart trembles, afraid of disobeying your word**  - AT: "I am shaking because I fear disobeying your word" (See: [[:kn:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]] and [[:kn:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification|Personification]])
