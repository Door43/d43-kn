===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The counsel of Yahweh goes out to those** - Possible meanings are 1) "Yahweh is a friend to those," 2) "Yahweh confides in those," or 3) "Yahweh counsels those." God's intimate counsel shows the intimate friendship he has with those who follow him.
  * **for he will free my feet from the net** - A net is a trap. Here it represents danger. AT: He will rescue me from danger" (See: [[:kn:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
