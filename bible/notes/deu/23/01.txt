===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **illegitimate child **- Possible meanings are 1) child born to a mother and father who are not married, or 2) one born of a harlot, or 3) any prohibited mixtures of man and woman (such as adultery or incest).
  * **to the tenth generation**  - to the many descendants coming after - The number ten marks completeness. (See: [[:kn:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
