===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **would not listen - ** did not pay attention, did not regard (See: [[:kn:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Yahweh your God turned the curse into a blessing for you **- 'Turned' means 'shifted' or 'changed' here. The incident refers to [[kn:bible:notes:num:23:11|Numbers 23:11]].
  * **peace or prosperity **- general welfare (See: [[:kn:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **during all your days**  - AT: "all your days forever"
