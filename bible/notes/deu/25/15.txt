===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **perfect and just weight…perfect and just measure**  - One situation would be that the grain sellers were not supposed to use false scales that showed that the amount of grain they were giving was greater than it really was. AT: "accurate and honest weight…accurate and honest measure"
